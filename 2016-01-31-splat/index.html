Splat!

January 31, 2016

Splat! is a little physics-based puzzle game for smarthphones with a minimalist and colorful design.

Gameplay

You have only limited number of splats(hits on white blocks) to reach the end of the level.

  • ◉ 2 levels of difficulty (normal and "beat the developer")
  • ◉ Compete with friends for highest score
  • ◉ It's totally free & no ads
