# 1. STUNNING GRAPHICS
Explore the beautifully minimalistic 3D design and futuristic graphics of the game world.

# 2. EASY CONTROLS
Drive sideways and collect beats. Choose your favorite controls (swipe or touch).

# 3. GREAT MUSIC
The game presents various music genre and popular songs, from pop music to electronic house.
For the best experience play with headphones and full volume.

![Break The Notes 1](/assets/img/projects/break_the_notes/2.jpg)

# How to play
- ◉ Collects beats and finish each level to unlock next one
- ◉ Challenge to get the perfect score and to be the first in highscores
- ◉ Unlock new spaceships or powerups for unranked games
- ◉ Play with your favorite songs from your smartphone
