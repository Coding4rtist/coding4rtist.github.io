***Vault is a local password manager, a simple app with a minimal style.***

# Why use Vault?
- ◉ With Vault you have to remember only 1 password.
- ◉ All the data is encrypted and saved only on your computer.
- ◉ Vault doesn't require internet connection.

![Vault 1](/assets/img/projects/vault_new/1.jpg)

# What are the differences from the old Vault?
- ◉ A completely new UI, even more minimal!
- ◉ Now you can store more than 1 account of the same website/app
- ◉ It's much more faster
- ◉ Bugfixes
